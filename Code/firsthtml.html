About the Hello World Initiative

Coding education should be free for everyone.

We are a not-for-profit community of coders who seek to facilitate learning while sharing our passion and love for code with others by utilizing open-source learning materials in a productive community environment.

It's dangerous to go alone! Here, take {HWI}.

The Hello World Initiative is community based

That means that we are a community of coders, by coders, for coders. We learn, teach, grow, and nerd out together! Whether it's for learning to code, beefing up your portfolio, or to make some awesome friends of code, everybody, newbies and veterans alike, have something to gain here.

Don't want to pay $15,000 for a coding bootcamp curriculum?

Good news - You don't have to!

Thanks to open-source, there is coding curriculum available for FREE online, and it is just as good (or better!) than the best bootcamps out there. Don't let the bootcamp industry fool you.
